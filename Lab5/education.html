My Education

Nanjing Zhonghua high school

My favorite class was definitely Maths class in my freshman and junior year

I went to high school at Nanjing Zhonghua high school and graduated in 2012. . At that time, I'm crazy about Math problems. Always giving up some other subjects and instead doing Math research. That made me have a bad grade in the college-entrance exam

Nanjing Xiaozhuang University

My favorite class was Linux system programming which made me learn a new system, and I could do researches based on this system.

I did my undergraduate studies at Nanjing Xiaozhuang University University and graduated with my Bachelors in Computer Science in 2012. To be honest, college life is awesome and color. There are many organizations in the university and their goal is to enhance student's communication each other. All the members will design some activities together.

University of North Dakota

My favorite class in UND is Artificial Intelligence because that's the latest technology

After my graduation in Nanjing Xiaozhuang University. I decided to make a deep study in my major. I definitely picked up USA as my destination because of advanced technology and facilities. I believe I have made a huge progress in my major during the period in UND. Now, I'm very familiar with American teaching and studying method. Regretly, I didn't complete my master at UND because of extremely strict degree requirement. Therefore, I transferred to Stevens in order to get a better learning about technology.
